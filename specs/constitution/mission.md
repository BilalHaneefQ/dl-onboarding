# Mission & Vision

> Part of the **Omnio** Product Constitution

**Last amended:** 2026-04-30

---

## Mission

Enable professionals to manage Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Meet through a single Discord DM, eliminating context-switching across fragmented productivity tools.

## Vision

A universal AI assistant layer where professionals control all their work tools through natural language in a single persistent messaging thread — regardless of which tool is underneath.

## Context

Professionals lose significant focus and time context-switching between Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Meet for routine tasks that shouldn't require opening three separate apps. The problem is acute for tasks that demand quick turnaround throughout the day — reading and replying to emails, rescheduling meetings, spinning up Meet links. Omnio replaces those context switches with a single Discord DM conversation, validated as a 2-week POC before any broader expansion.
